The Frame · what the axes mean

Two measurable axes → four kinds of opportunity

Instead of one opaque score, every title is placed by source strength (public readership & momentum) and optionality (how many distinct-audience formats it can carry). Where it lands tells you how to play it.

FRANCHISE
high source · high optionality
Big original that credibly carries 3 formats. Build the whole franchise — drama, film, anime. Highest ceiling, most competition.
NICHE MULTI
lower source · high optionality
Smaller original but broad fit. A low-cost multi-format play — spread small bets across audiences.
TENTPOLE
high source · narrow optionality
Big original that fits one format strongly. A single concentrated bet — usually anime for action IP.
PASS
lower source · narrow fit
Neither big nor broad. Not worth an option right now.
